Album Reviews

Below is a review of each studio album released by Fontaines D.C.

Album Release Year Review
Dogrel 2019 A raw and energetic debut album packed with sharp lyrics and infectious post-punk rhythms. It captures the spirit of modern Dublin while introducing the band's distinctive storytelling style.
A Hero's Death 2020 A darker and more reflective follow-up that explores anxiety, identity and personal growth. The slower pace highlights the band's evolving songwriting.
Skinty Fia 2022 Often considered their masterpiece, Skinty Fia explores themes of Irish identity and displacement through atmospheric production and emotional depth.
Romance 2024 The band's most ambitious album to date. Romance embraces cinematic sounds, dream-pop influences and vulnerable songwriting while remaining unmistakably Fontaines D.C.
