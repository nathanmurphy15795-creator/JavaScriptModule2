Album Artwork

Fontaines D.C. have consistently used striking artwork to complement the themes within each album. Their covers evolve alongside their music, reflecting changes in mood, identity and artistic direction.

Dogrel (2019)

The bold minimalist design reflects the band's energetic debut. Like the songs themselves, the artwork feels direct, gritty and unapologetically Irish.

A Hero's Death (2020)

The surreal image perfectly represents the album's themes of mental health, self-doubt and personal growth.

Skinty Fia (2022)

The striking deer imagery reflects Irish folklore, identity, migration and cultural belonging.

Romance (2024)

Romance embraces softer colours and cinematic imagery, mirroring the band's increasingly expansive musical style.
