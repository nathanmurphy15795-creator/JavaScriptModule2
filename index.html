Welcome

Welcome to my website dedicated to reviewing the music of Fontaines D.C. This site explores their studio albums, artwork and musical evolution from their debut through to Romance.

About the Band

Fontaines D.C. have become one of Ireland's most influential modern rock bands. Their music blends post-punk energy with poetic lyricism, creating albums that explore identity, politics, love and life in contemporary Ireland.

Featured Album

A Hero's Death

Released in 2020, A Hero's Death marked a significant change in the band's sound. Instead of relying on the raw aggression of Dogrel, the album adopted a slower, darker and more introspective approach.
